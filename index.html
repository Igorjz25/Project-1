    Usual Day

    The usual day of Russian student begins from waking up in 7 AM and dressing up into school uniform and doing all morning activities. Then he goes to school in 8 am and the lessons starts 8:30. The student is coming in, and giving his coat to the cloakroom worker, and getting plate with a number. Then he goes to his classes. The Homeroom classmates are traveling are traveling with him to all classes. Then in the end of the day he goes to cafeteria, and having a lunch, after that he gives his plate with number to the cloackroom worker and goes home.
